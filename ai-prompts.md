# Next · 网站视觉素材 AI 生图提示词

> 使用工具：建议 Midjourney V6 / DALL-E 3 / Stable Diffusion XL
> 风格统一：深色电影基调 + 暖金色点缀 + 极简精致

---

## 一、Logo 品牌标识（最重要）

**用途：** 网站左上角品牌标识 + 浏览器 favicon
**建议比例：** 1:1 正方形
**推荐工具：** Midjourney 或 DALL-E 3

### 提示词（Primary）

```
A minimalist wordmark logo for a movie recommendation website called "Next". 
The word "Next" in a modern sans-serif font, bold weight, letter "N" integrated with a subtle cinema film reel element or a beam of projector light.
Color palette: deep dark background (#0A0A0F), warm gold text (#D4A855).
Clean, premium, cinematic feel. No extra shapes or icons.
Vector-style, flat design, high contrast. 
White space around the text, centered composition.
--ar 1:1 --style raw --v 6
```

### 备用提示词（纯文字标）

```
A clean, minimalist wordmark "Next" in bold sans-serif typeface.
Warm gold color (#D4A855) on transparent background.
Cinema-inspired, elegant, modern.
The letter "x" extends slightly to suggest forward motion.
No background, no embellishments, just the word.
Vector style, high resolution, brand identity design.
--ar 1:1 --v 6
```

## 二、Hero 背景氛围图（首页大图底纹）

**用途：** 首页顶部大推荐区域的背景氛围
**建议比例：** 16:9 横版（1920×1080）
**数量：** 2张，交替使用

### 提示词①（电影院氛围）

```
Interior of an empty vintage cinema theater, warm amber and deep burgundy color palette, velvet theater seats in soft focus foreground, a large blank movie screen in the center of the composition, warm beam of projector light cutting through the dark space, cinematic film grain texture, moody atmosphere, rich shadows, dramatic lighting, 8K quality, photorealistic, wide angle shot, the center of the screen area should be clear and empty (for text overlay).
--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6
```

### 提示词②（光影抽象）

```
Abstract cinematic composition, a beam of warm golden light piercing through darkness, subtle film reel elements fading into shadow, deep navy and warm amber color palette, film grain texture, vignette effect, moody and atmospheric, minimalist but rich, the center of the image is relatively empty dark space (for text overlay), professional photography quality, 8K.
--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6
```

```
A beautifully designed placeholder image for a movie poster that is unavailable.

Overall feeling: premium, not cheap. Hotel-luxury-card style minimalism.
Dark ambient mood, cinematic, no complex details.

```

**用途：** 用户将网站添加到手机桌面时显示的图标
**建议比例：** 1:1 正方形（1024×1024px）

### 提示词

```
A stunning app icon for a movie recommendation app called "Next".
Deep dark background (#0A0A0F), a stylized glowing golden letter "N" in the center.
The "N" has a subtle cinema-related detail — one leg of the letter extends into a thin line suggesting a film strip or projector light beam.
Surrounding the letter, very subtle floating golden particles.
Icon should look like a premium streaming service app icon.
Clean, memorable, high-end. No text, just the letter "N".
Rounded square crop, iOS style app icon proportions.
--ar 1:1 --v 6
```

Dark cinematic atmosphere, warm amber and deep blue color palette, abstract composition of light and shadow suggesting a movie theater lobby or backstage area, very dark overall (80% black), subtle reflections, the frame should be dark enough for white text to be readable when overlaid, film grain, moody, elegant, quiet luxury feel. ## 制作建议

| 素材 | 优先级 | 影响最大 |
|:----|:-----:|:--------|
| 🔴 **Logo** | **最优先** | 品牌识别，所有页面都会看到 |
| 🟡 **Hero背景** | 次优先 | 首页第一屏的视觉冲击 |
| 🟢 **缺省海报占位** | 第三优先 | 偶尔出现，但影响体验 |
| 🟢 **App Icon** | 可有可无 | 手机添加到桌面才用到 |
| 🟢 **登录页背景** | 最后 | 登录页不然直接用纯色也行 |

**建议顺序：** Logo → Hero背景 → 缺省海报 → App Icon → 登录背景
